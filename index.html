The Honest Take
🏆
Marbella: no single weakness
Scores 7.8+ across every single priority. The most reliable choice for a group with varied interests.
⛳
Golf rules out Mykonos
Mykonos has zero courses on the island. If golf matters at all, Marbella is the only answer.
🎉
Mykonos wins peak moments
Beach clubs, boat trips and nightlife scenes that genuinely can't be matched anywhere else in the Med.
💰
Marbella wins on value
Mykonos in peak season is brutal — €200 watermelons at Nammos exist. Marbella is luxurious without the same shock.
